About Us

Malilani Global Metals Ltd has been a trusted name in the mining and metal export industry for years. With hundreds of satisfied clients and glowing testimonials, we specialize in the extraction, processing, and export of high-quality minerals including Copper, Gold, Cobalt, Coltan, and many others, both processed and unprocessed. Headquartered in the United Kingdom, with a strategic branch in Dubai, we are committed to excellence and sustainability in global metal markets.
